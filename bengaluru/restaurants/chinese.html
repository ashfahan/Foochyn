    Eat Light, Feel Light

    Chinese Cuisine

    Chinese cuisine is one of the frontrunners in fine dining circuit in India. Many great fancy restaurants dedicate their attention towards this glorious exotic fare, catering to the Chinese food lovers all across the country. When it comes to Chinese food fandom, Bengaluru is no different. The city hosts some marvelous Chinese restaurants you can’t wait to explore. Ruling the roost is an expat, ‘Yauatcha’, who with it’s sleek modern designs and drool-worthy Chinese food, pulls great many foodies to it. If you want fancy and contemporary, Yauatcha is your best bet. It’s located in 1MG Road Mall, MG Road and is counted as one of the best Chinese restaurants in Bengaluru. Next comes the ever popular, ‘Mainland China’, a string of family-style Chinese eateries known for serving five star quality Chinese food for reasonable prices. It’s outlet in Orion Mall, Malleshwaram is a sought after one.

    Sometimes Chinese food has to share it’s platform with other cuisines as well, without dimming it’s shine. ‘District 6’ is the perfect example. This trendy attractive microbrewery situated at Rajajinagar of West Bengaluru is the ideal spot to kick off your heels and indulge in soy-enhanced fare along with a chilled beer. Although Chinese food is not exclusive here, it still manages to hold its ground with strong flavours. If it’s affordable Chinese food you want, you should definitely check out ‘Kitchen on Table’ in HSR. This is a lovely Pan Asian restaurant, which comes with a Teppanyaki table and all the theatrics. Chinese food is one of the popular cuisines they serve and is as authentic as it can get. No alcohol is served here but give a shot to their buffets.

    Infuse some glamour to your date or finesse to your business meeting by booking a table at the stylish ‘The Lantern Restaurant & Bar’. The sheer elegance of the restaurant is perfectly foiled by their authentic Chinese menu. Housed under The Ritz Carlton, The Lantern Restaurant & Bar is one of the loveliest restaurants in Bengaluru. Tap into it for flawless service, sleek contemporary interiors and delightful Chinese food. If you are high on glamour and don’t mind splurging then ‘Shang Palace’ would be another ideal pick. This Shangri-La Hotel fixture is renowned for it’s sophisticated interiors and pure Chinese food. Be it a fancy date or an important wine & dine of a client, Shang Palace would be ideal for both.
