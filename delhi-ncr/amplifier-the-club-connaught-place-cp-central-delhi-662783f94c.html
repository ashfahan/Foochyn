    Phantom Speaks Secret Restaurant Reviewer

    4.5/5

    This multi-cuisine restaurant and pub is in Connaught Place, New Delhi. The brick-walled themed interiors along with wooden dcor give this place both chic and elegant vibe to party with friends or family. The place is huge with a comfortable seating area. Amplifier The Club specializes in Indian, Continental and Oriental cuisine. Begin your culinary journey here with their mouth-watering starters Turkish Style Grilled Chicken on Skewers, Paneer Tikka or Crispy Salt N Pepper Vegetables. Slurp a heady Red Berry Daiquiri or Margarita on the Rocks alongside your food. For the main course, you must opt for Grilled Fish with Lemon Butter Sauce, Chicken with Garlic Basil Sauce served with Steamed Rice, Kadhai Paneer, Butter Chicken or Dal Makhani. Chill with your friends while you enjoy finger-licking food along with great music.
