    As we said before, about the glamour quotient of Taj City Centre Gurugram, Culina 44 reflects the same grandeur through its decor. The coffee shop is designed in a unique way, with high rising ceiling and plenty of use of glass. One entire wall comprises of clear glass peeking outside into the courtyard where few outdoor tables are added for the patrons to laze at.
