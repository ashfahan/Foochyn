    Critic Rating

    Amit Speaks Writer & Journalist

    4.2/5

    A massive restaurant running the length of Foodhall and then some, MKT takes its multi-cuisine positioning rather seriously. Nearly every section of the menu gets its own chefs and cooking stations, much like Set’z. All of it is held together by peppy service and value-for-money pricing. I suggest menu recommendations from the manager or the chefs manning the open-kitchens. Once in a while, a kitchen sent across a plate that just is not quite up there. Like my overcooked quarter of a 'Rotisserie Chicken' and a fairly unexciting serving of 'Crispy Lamb'. The inconsistent form seeps into the dessert section as well, churning out great ice cream from fresh ingredients and a benchmark 'New York Cheesecake' sometime and then sending me a slice of 'Nutella Chocolate Ganache Frozen' hard enough to use as a paperweight.

    About MKT, The Chanakya


    MKT in Chanakya is an upscale restaurant located in the heart of the capital city- New Delhi. Offering an expansive assortment of international food, serving over 15 cuisines under one roof, the place is truly every gourmand’s paradise. The restaurant has benchmarked the highest degree of excellence across the f&b industry.

    The well-appointed interiors done by best-in-class designers have divided the entire area into interactive live kitchens surrounded by a cozy and formal seating arrangement.

    MKT offers a world class dining experience by serving an excellent buffet. The scrumptious selection of food offered here ranges across North Indian, Southern Coastal, Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Thai, Malaysian and street food, among others. Every delicacy is prepared using authentic recipes and flavorful ingredients that impart a rich texture to it. All the live kitchens are manned by different chefs who are well-aware of the intricacies of every cuisine.

    The food offering gets even more creative with their exquisite Mediterranean food choices that include many European delicacies such as grills, cold cuts, roasts, pastas and pizzas. The live pizza oven offers mouthwatering pizzas, crusty and charred at the edges, tossed in a delicious sauce and garnished with high-quality toppings that taste much more than just dry vegetables or thawed meat. Dishes like Fish Tikka, River Sole Chettinad Tawa Fry, Pork Chops, Enchiladas, and Sushi are pretty good. Lastly, their assortment of ice creams as well as the New York Cheesecake will help you end your meal on the most delightful note. You can also complement your food with your choice of alcohol served here.

    So, if you are looking to visit a good restaurant at a central location and experience a luxury feel, you must book your table at MKT. With a live kitchen concept, excellent service and an international menu at your disposal, you are bound to feel overwhelmed. However, it must be noted that the place is slightly expensive. But you can leverage our online portal to score some good deals on food and beverages to make your dining experience much more affordable.
