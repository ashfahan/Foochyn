    Phantom Speaks Secret Restaurant Reviewer

    3.9/5

    SGF - Spice Grill Flame is Indias fastest growing pure vegetarian restaurant chain with more than 30+ outlets across India. Known for its lip-smacking North Indian food and special expertise in Soy products, SGF focuses on providing taste, quality, and flavorful vegetarian meal options. With its extensive menu offering more than 100 delectable dishes, this unique outlet provides veg equivalents of several primarily non-vegetarian delicacies. For instance, their 'Veg Wings' and 'Veg Lollypop' are a sumptuous and huge hit amongst Vegetarian foodies. Their variety of "chaaps" and "paneer dishes" is what draws most vegetarian people to this place while their Daal Makhni, Dahi Ke Kebab, and Tawa Tikka Masala are delicacies that are unanimously loved.
