    Susmita Speaks Independent journalist

    4.1/5

    United Coffee House harks back to a time when not many could access the finer things of life. Today dining out has been democratised beyond our wildest imagination and the restaurant stands testimony to these transformative experiences. Upholstered wall panels, vintage chandeliers and glass panelled doors transport you back to the days of the British Raj as frenetic Connaught Place recedes to a blur outside. Put up your feet in style on the plush leather sofas, even as you savour the old world charm of a bygone era. The restaurant opens its doors for breakfast and you have the option to choose anything from 'Cold Meats' and 'Baked Beans' to 'Stuffed Paranthas' and 'Mysore Dosa'. Despite the menu boasting of serious Continental main course items, the light bites offer a stiff competition.

    About United Coffee House, Connaught Place (CP), Central Delhi


    With the moving time as most restaurants are heading towards the new and modern, there are some left grasping the old with both hands. Not everyone is rushing to transform themselves into another contemporary reproduction. Few are holding back to what they call their vintage best. There are several restaurants in Delhi NCR still carrying on its classic vibe and enchanting the diners with a glimpse into the era gone by. United Coffee House is doing exactly that. It has maintained its original dcor the way it was in 1942 when it opened its doors for the first time. Sprawled on ground floor level in Connaught Place, New Delhi, this famous coffee house is an oldie but a good one. It still has a huge base of longstanding patrons coming in religiously for their coffee fix.

    United Coffee House is one of the legendary outlets that has been around for ages. It has more than 80 years of service under its belt and is known to serve an exhaustive multi-cuisine menu in a lovely vintage dcor. Treat yourself and your companion to a luxurious menu of, North Indian, European, Asian and Mediterranean cuisines. The restaurant opens little after 9 am so one can stop by for breakfast before heading for work. United Coffee House is an ideal place for a delightful supper with a friend. Begin the meal with a set of appetizers like, pulled pork tacos, vegetable sui mai, kaale zaitoon ka murg tikka, spicy Hunan prawns and keema samosa. As your second course consider having, chicken a la kiev, nargis ke kofte, Portuguese fish medallions, multani champein and five spice claypot duck.

    The menu is truly vast. One trip wont be enough to sample everything. Let the chef tempt your sweet tooth with his delicious sugary concoctions. At your fingertips will be, shahi halwa tart, gluten free almond & orange cake, chocolate decadence, kala jaam, red velvet gateaux, apple pie tatin and New York based cheese cake. If you book with us at EazyDiner, we will be able to give you amazing deals and discounts. United Coffee House still looks the same as it was in 1942. Plush carpet beneath your feet and glittering chandelier hanging overhead gives you an inkling how the restaurant looked so many decades ago. Intricate wall moldings painted with gold and long leather sofas further adds to the grandeur.
