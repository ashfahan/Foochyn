    Critic Rating

    Phantom Speaks Secret Restaurant Reviewer

    4.1/5

    With outlets all across India, this place is known for serving some of the best waffles. The interiors are bright with yellow dcor and wooden seating. This place serves waffles in both sweet and savoury options. The savoury ones available here come in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian choices which is an uncommon and interesting thing to feast on. Apart from the usual 'Nutella' and 'Marshmallow', they have a Fruit Waffle with Seasonal Fruits, a Granola' and 'Dry Fruit waffle. Some of the savoury options that are must-try are their Brekkie Egg Waffle, Spinach with Chicken or the Chicken Keema Wala. You can also try out some of their fries and wedges if you are not a waffle fan, such as their Cheesy Fries, Peri Peri Wedges or Chipotle Fries. Go for the Oreo & Cream with Nutella as it is one of their best sellers.
