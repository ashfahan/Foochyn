    About What A Comic Show, SDA, South Delhi


    Restauranteurs are cleverly targeting the youths by luring them with quirky concepts and fun Insta-worthy decors. They are coming up with playful ideas to attract the young college goers who prefer to hang around eateries for hours on end chatting and eating. All it takes is some creative décor, quirky theme and a cozy set up to make them feel right at home. ‘What A Comic Show’ brings out the child in you through it’s comic-central décor, which does a great job of attracting the students from nearby institutions. Quite aptly located, on the 2nd Floor, SDA Market, SDA, New Delhi, the cafe enjoys the hustle-bustle of this buzzing neighborhood. The casual colorful set up of the café makes it an ideal spot to chill out between classes or complete the pending notes.

    Menu of What A Comic Show is curated keeping the eclectic tastes of youths in mind. Their varied unpredictable mood calls for a menu that is all encompassing as well as comforting. The delicious combination of Continental, Italian and Chinese cuisines goes down well with the punters. They do serve wine and beer to quench your parched throat. Idea is to encourage you to linger so plenty of munchies on the menu. Dig into, fire meatballs, chicken sausage bacon bites, mushroom caps, spicy chicken wings, bruschetta, potato cheese nuggets, honey bbq pork ribs, golden fried prawn, smoked lamb basil, fish plachod, chilly paneer dry and double fried pork. Any leftover appetite could be satisfied with, prawn butter dumplings, vegetable chopsuey, chicken pan fried noodles, shredded pork in spring onion & chilly, BLT hotdog, spinach & corn sandwich, shell shock pasta and tenderloin steak.

    Add to your waist with a round of luscious desserts served at What A Comic Show. Polish off, waffle with ice cream, Nutella pancake with ice cream, chocolate cake, brownie with ice cream, strawberry waffle with ice cream, banana pancake with ice cream and chocolate cake with ice cream. The café organizes live entertainment once in a while to keep the diners happy. Food is inexpensive but for even more reasonable bill book with EazyDiner and get amazing discounts. Tables are set a little close by. The lack of space is neutralized by the use of vivid yellow color. Comic printed lamps, wall art and tabletops re-emphasizes the comic theme. Décor is vibrant, youthful and creative. Grab a table in their balcony section if possible.
