    About Nine7One, The Oberoi, Dubai


    If you are looking for an awesome place to hang out with your friends on a Friday and Saturday night in Dubai, you just need to walk in Nine7One .Tucked in the opulent environs of Lower Lobby Level of the impressive Hotel Oberoi, Business Bay, Dubai Nine7One, is a fantastic bar cum lounge. With its trendy and sophisticated interiors, the modern bar and lounge exhibits a rejuvenating ambience. You can enjoy captivating water views through its full-length windows Its magical aura and airy ambience captures your heart and soul. It is the perfect place to unwind over vibrant music and wine

    Savour drinks, delectable Italian, and European .food. You can choose from an array of cocktails, single malts, premium wines, delicious cocktails, and artisan spirits. Nine7One offers interesting weekly brunches with tangy mock tails, sheesha, dry martini, kebab platter including fish, chicken and mutton, yummy pasta, juicy burgers, chicken biryani, rogan josh and delectable desserts such as Caramelia Crunch .Pesto sauce, Pan Seared Seabass in Tomato Basil sauce, and Egyptian Chicken is worth trying. Prawns with pineapple salad are yummy. Music is very energizing and ideal to its magical environment. Open from 12:30 PM to 3 PM and, 7 PM to 11. A meal for two costs AED 300 for two people

    The lively atmosphere of the Nine7One makes it a special place where young people can spend long moments of sheer fun. The service is quick. The waiters are helpful and polite. Well-mannered and courteous waiters serve the delectable finger food and accompanied spirits with style. The restaurant is ideal for a romantic rendezvous. With music, playing in the backdrop you can spend the best time with your friends or family. An evening in Nine7One is a treat where you can enjoy delicious food and fabulous cocktails served with chic style. A energetic and interactive evening in Nine7One with your friends will leave a distinct memory. You would love to visit this alluring place for New Year celebrations or for celebrating important events of your life and at every given chance. You can book your table in advance.
