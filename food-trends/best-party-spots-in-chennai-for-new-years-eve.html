    New Year Eve Spots To Suit Every Taste

    24 Dec, 2019 by Amit Patnaik

    It is a once-in-a-decade NYE in a few days. That should be reason enough to wine and dine even harder and grander. Like every year, we are back with a cheat-sheet of the best dining and party spots in the city. But this year, let’s take things to another level with the top 20 spots in Chennai to bring in 2020.


    The Lady Connemara Bar & Lounge, Taj Connemara, Chennai

    Raise a toast to the new year as Lady Connemara might have in 1799, at this iconic Chennai hotel, recently renovated by Taj for this decade.

    NYE Deal: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, Starters for Single Entry 

    ₹2999 ₹ 2699 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 08:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 12:30 AM


    Prego, Taj Coromandel, Chennai

    Keep it classy this NYE with live music, fine Italian dining and Chennai’s biggest selection of wine at Prego.

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Live Performance/ Music, Set Menu for Single Entry ₹ 4000 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, Set Menu for Single Entry  ₹ 5000 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 06:30 PM to 31 Dec 2019 11:30 PM
     

    Kipling Cafe, Kipling, East Coast Road (ECR)

    Nestled in a lush seaside garden, Kipling Café makes for a cosy yet trendy party spot. And you can walk down to the well-maintained Neelankarai beach in a minute. 

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited IMFL, Unlimited Non Alcoholic Beverages, Live Performance/ Music, DJ Party, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry ₹ 3700 (Including all taxes) 

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited IMFL, Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Couple ₹ 5700 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 08:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 12:30 AM


    The Verandah, Taj Connemara, Chennai

    Recreate yesteryear charm with a pool-side dinner and foot-tapping music at this legendary Chennai institution.

    NYE Deal1: New Year Brunch - Veg Buffet, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Single Entry ₹2600 ₹ 2340 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal3: New Year Brunch - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Veg Buffet, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Single Entry ₹3750 ₹ 3375 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: Valid from 01 Jan 2020 11:30 PM to 01 Jan 2020 03:30 PM

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - DJ Party, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry  ₹2750 ₹ 2475 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal4: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, DJ Party, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry ₹4250 ₹ 3825 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: Valid from 31 Dec 2019 07:30 PM to 01 Jan 2020 12:30 AM


    The Leather Bar, The Park, Chennai

    The Leather Bar was the bar to see and be seen in the early years of this decade and has retained its cult-like following among Chennai’s bar hoppers. 


    Anise, Taj Coromandel, Chennai

    Located in the leafy city-centre of Nungabakkam, Anise is one of Chennai’s top spots for family dinners.

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry ₹ 4000 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Candle Light Dinner, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry  ₹ 5000 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:30 PM to 31 Dec 2019 11:30 PM


    Mechanics, Nungambakkam, Chennai

    Out with the old, in with the new! This brand-new techno-grunge themed bar is one of the hottest in town.

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, DJ Party, Starters for Single Lady ₹ 1499 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, DJ Party, Starters for Male Stag

    ₹ 2499 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal3: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, DJ Party, Starters for Couple

    ₹ 3999 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec  2019 07:30 PM to 01 Jan 202012:30 AM


    Clubhouse - All Day Dining Restaurant, Taj Club House, Chennai

    Bring on the cheer with plates piled high and hips swaying to the music at the classic Taj Clubhouse on Mount Road.

    NYE Deal1: New Year Brunch - Veg Buffet, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music, Starters for Single Entry ₹ 2000 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year Brunch - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Veg Buffet, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music, Starters for Single Entry  ₹ 2600 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 01 Jan 2020 12:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 03:30 PM

    NYE Deal3: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, Starters, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry  ₹ 3500 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:30 PM to 31 Dec 2019 11:30 PM


    Beyond Indus, Taj Club House, Chennai

    Fancy a Butter Chicken and Dal Makhani kind of NYE? Then grab the NYE-special Thali at Beyond Indus. 

    NYE Deal: New Year's Eve Dinner - Thali for Single Entry ₹ 2200 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:00 PM to 31 Dec 2019 11:30 PM


    Kayal, Le Royal Meridien, Chennai

    If a seafood-fiesta on 2019’s last dinner sounds like your scene, then Kayal has one of Chennai’s best spreads.

    NYE Deal1: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Non Alcoholic Beverages, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Child ₹3000 ₹ 1815 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Live Performance/ Music, Premium Liquor, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Male Stag ₹6050 ₹ 3650 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal3: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Couple  ₹12100 ₹ 6050 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 12:00 AM


    Navaratna, Le Royal Meridien, Chennai

    Celebrate New Year’s in a gorgeously bedecked spot with live traditional Indian music and delicious eats from across the country at Navaratna. 

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Child ₹ 1299 (Including all taxes) 

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry ₹ 2499 (Including all taxes) 

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 01:00 AM 


    Score Bar & Grill, Holiday Inn Chennai OMR IT Expressway

    Conveniently located near both Velachery and OMR, this sports-themed bar will be an ideal home ground to party away NYE. 

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Food, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Dance floor with DJ, Live Performance/ Music, Starters for Male Stag ₹ 3599 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Food, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Dance floor with DJ, Live Performance/ Music for Couple ₹ 5999 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 08:00 PM to 31 Dec 2019 11:30 PM


    Boats, Besant Nagar, Chennai

    Party away the night at this beach-side bar and catch 2020’s first dawn. One of the few sea-view venues within Chennai city.


    OPM 2.0, The Accord Metropolitan, Chennai

    Located on the Accord Metropolitan’s rooftop with sweeping views of T Nagar, OPM 2.0 has the perfect vibe to bring in 2020 under the stars.

    NYE Deal1: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, DJ Party, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry  ₹3456 ₹ 3110  (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, DJ Party, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Male Stag ₹4567 ₹ 4110 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 08:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 12:00 AM


    Wire Room, Phoenix Market City, Chennai

    If you are planning on shopping for year-end deals before partying away the night, then Wire Room at Chennai’s top mall is the place to be.

    NYE Deal1: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Food, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Dance floor with DJ Party for Single Lady ₹2499 ₹ 2249 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Food, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Dance floor with DJ Party for Male Stag ₹2999 ₹ 2699 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal3: Early Bird New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Food, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Dance floor with DJ Party for Couple ₹4499 ₹ 4049 (Including all taxes) 

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 08:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 01:00 AM


    The Dome, Le Royal Meridien, Chennai

    With a great collection of malt and cigars, The Dome should be a gentleman’s choice for welcoming 2020 with pizzas.

    NYE Deal1: New Year's Eve Dinner - Starters, Domestic Liquor for Single Entry  ₹ 1799 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal2: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Starters for Single Entry  ₹ 2499  (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 06:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 01:00 AM


    Cilantro, Le Royal Meridien, Chennai

    Eat well and be merry with your loved ones on New Year’s Eve with a special spread, live music and unlimited pours of libation at Cilantro.

    NYE Deal1: New Year Brunch - Veg Buffet, Unlimited Non Alcoholic Beverages, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Child ₹ 1020 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 01 Jan 2020 11:30 AM to 01 Jan 2020 03:30 PM

    NYE Deal2: New Year Brunch - Veg Buffet, Unlimited Non Alcoholic Beverages, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Single Entry ₹ 2020 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 01 Jan 2020 11:30 AM to 01 Jan 2020 03:00 PM

    NYE Deal3: New Year Brunch - Veg Buffet, Unlimited Imported Liquor, Non Veg Buffet, Live Performance/ Music for Single Entry ₹ 2940 (Including all taxes)

    Validity: 01 Jan 2020 11:30 AM to 01 Jan 2020 03:30 PM

    NYE Deal4: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Non Alcoholic Beverages, Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Child  ₹ 1820 (Including all taxes)

    NYE Deal5: New Year's Eve Dinner - Unlimited Imported Liquor, Live Performance/ Music, Veg Dinner Buffet, Non-veg Dinner Buffet for Single Entry ₹ 3020 (Including all taxes) 

    Validity: 31 Dec 2019 07:00 PM to 01 Jan 2020 01:00 AM


    Pool Party, Le Royal Meridien, Chennai

    A pool-side party with a rocking DJ, heady cocktails and lip-smacking eats is as fancy as it gets for New Year’s Eve

    Aqua, The Park, Chennai

    One of Chennai’s best rooftop poolside venues, Aqua is a no-brainer with its signature cabanas, hookah service and trendy vibe.
