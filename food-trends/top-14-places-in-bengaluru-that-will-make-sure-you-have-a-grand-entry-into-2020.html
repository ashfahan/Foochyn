    • Top 14 Places in Bengaluru That Will Make Sure You have a Grand Entry into 2020

    A New Year’s Party with family, or friends, stags or single ladies – Bengaluru offers something for everyone

    29 Dec, 2019 by Ruth DSouza Prabhu

    A Memorable Welcome to New Year with Novotel Bengaluru Outer Ring Road

    If your idea of welcoming the New Year is to indulge in some quality time with your family over a meal cooked to perfection by the poolside, then Blue Terrain is the place for you. Bid adieu to 2019 at Novotel Bengaluru Outer Ring Road and celebrate with a splendid dinner under the starry night sky that is sure to make it a memorable New Year’s Eve. Indulge in the delicacies crafted by culinary artists featuring Pork Porchetta, Shepherd’s Pie, Beef Wellington, Waffle Sandwiches and more! The tantalizing cocktails and exclusive wines will take your experience up a notch as you revel in the perfect ambience.

    Where: Blue Terrain, Novotel Bengaluru Outer Ring Road

    When: 31st December 2019

    Time: 08:00 PM onwards



    Party with Lights at Conrad Bengaluru

    Sing & Jive by the poolside at Tiamo

    Spend a breezy poolside under a starlit night, sparkling gold class high spirits, European and Mediterranean live food counters and personal butler at every table, which is sure to ignite your senses. Let champagne low and passion soar as DJ Arjun and Goatronicks strike right chords for celebration. 

    When: 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:30 PM onwards

    31st Night F&B Package

    Inclusions: Butler service, premium liquor like Gold Label, Mediterranean live food counter, dance under the starlit sky.

    World Cuisine at Conrad Bengaluru

    This New Year, welcome to an eclectic combination of world cuisine across 3 award-winning restaurants - Indian Durbar, Mikusu and Caraway Kitchen) at Conrad Bengaluru. Make it a night to remember. Experience a whole new atmosphere at The Lobby Brew, while it transforms into an LED zone. Swing to the beat the live band - Family Band and continue to party into the night with the new age beats of DJ Manu. Let the bold new experience leading to the New Year bedazzle you.

    When: 31st December 2019, 07:30 PM onwards

    31st Night F&B Package 

    Inclusions: Hop across Mikusu, Indian Durbar and Caraway Kitchen with access to The Lobby dance floor


    New Year at Sheraton Grand Bangalore Hotel at Brigade Gateway

    Head to Sheraton Grand Bangalore Hotel at Brigade Gateway for these luxurious New Year Eve Parties. The hotel will bring in the perfect amalgamation of music and food and beverages to suit every taste. Experience some pulsating dance music by celebrity DJs at the highest rooftop lounge for the ardent party goers, there is live jazz music for the quiet ones and open sky dinner for two or family.

    Make your New Year's Eve a memorable one with your loved ones and ring in 2020. And, in case you are worried about your little rockstars then don’t be, Sheraton Grand Bangalore Hotel at Brigade Gateway has taken care of it by curating a special party zone for the little people for them to enjoy.

    Take a look at the parties and be a part of:

    1. Bollywood Blockbuster at Grand Ballroom – Groove to the desi beats and bring the New Year with some Bollywood Dhamaka played by celebrity Danish Seth and DJ Mohsin.

    Date: 31st December 2019

    Venue: Grand Ballroom

    Time: 08:00 PM onwards

    2. Adieu 2019 at Bene, Alfresco by Bene and The Persian Terrace - End the year in style with all-inclusive access for New Year’s Eve revelry at our award-winning restaurants with all the best beats spinning by renowned DJ Raghu Achar.

    Date: 31st December 2019

    Venue: Bene, Alfresco By Bene and The Persian Terrace

    Time: 08:00 PM onwards


    3. FLY HIGH at HIGH Ultra Lounge – Celebrate New Year eve at 421ft above ground level with the best commercial DJ in town with DJ Martin D’souza.


    Date: 31st December 2019

    Venue: HIGH Ultra Lounge

    Time: 08:00 PM onwards.

    You can also opt for some of their staycation packages.


    This bundled package invites the guests to celebrate the New Year with access to the Bollywood DJ night at the Grand Ballroom along with an unlimited buffet dinner.




    The simultaneous offer in the hotel is a set buffet dinner at The Persian Terrace, Bene and Alfresco by Bene with a serving of unlimited alcohol along with live music and access to the DJ night and last but not the least, at 421 feet highest rooftop plush lounge at HIGH Ultra Lounge.


    AURA 2020 – Kickstart the New Year with a bang at Courtyard by Marriott Bengaluru

    Courtyard by Marriott Bengaluru is back with Aura 2.0 that promises a memorable New Year's Eve. A package that is not just designed to bid 2019 adieu in style but ring in the new year in comfort with a stay after the party and a check out after breakfast buffet! Poolside dining, world-class mixology, live dhol with DJs and Celebrity Artists, unlimited food and beverage, we have got it all covered to make sure this New Year's Eve ends on a high note for you!

    When: 31st December 2019

    Time: 08:00 PM onwards


    Ring in 2020 with Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel

    Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel brings to you an incredible line up of exciting activities to make this New Year one to remember. New Year is a grand affair, from making resolutions, buying gifts for the family and friends to sending New Year greetings to near and dear ones.


    Ring in the New Year in the lap of luxury at the hotel as they offer you two different New Year Packages, which you are sure to enjoy.


    Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel offers the New Year Party Package for the 31st Evening to kick start the decade with good food and merry-making and groove to the music of DJ Jasmeet and DJ Danny. The New Year party is spread across four different enigmatic venues of the hotel - the Lawn, R Bar, Cabana and Lush which comes in an exciting package. The hotel further offers two Stay Packages – for its guests to indulge in some family time and be part of the New Year’s Eve. Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel is all set to pamper you with their exotic New Year Brunch.

    When: 31st December 2019



    Ring in the New Year 2020 in style at Grand Mercure Bangalore

    As we bid adieu to 2019 and welcome the cheer of a new decade, raise a toast to fresh beginnings over a delectable spread at Grand Mercure Bangalore. Begin your New Year feasting on scrumptious delights from 12th Main and By the Blue that offer the best delicacies to indulge on.

    A delectable New Year’s feast at 12th Main

    An elegant sit-down dinner for friends & family to reminiscence the memories of the year gone by & welcome the New Year. Head to 12th Main for a lavish multi-cuisine buffet that offers unlimited food with alcoholic & non-alcoholic beverages.


    Celebrate the year ending with dishes like Hariyali Fish Tikka, Murgh Banjara, Crumbed Fried Fish n House Chips, Paneer Tikka and more.

    Venue: 12th Main

    Date: Tuesday, 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:00 PM onwards

    Call in the New Year’s Eve in Bohemian style at By The Blue

    Grand Mercure and Suuz Party presents the most happening New Year’s Eve Poolside Party of the year at By The Blue. Begin with sparkling wine and dig into an array of specially curated menu items such as Crumbed Fried Chicken Bites, Pan-seared Prawns, Lucknowi Chicken Dum Biryani and extensive choices of desserts. The evening will be accompanied by foot-tapping music and a Bohemian styled set-up. Come and celebrate the new beginnings as you dance to the tune of well-renowned DJ Javed.

    Venue: By The Blue

    Date: 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:00 PM onwards



    Ring in the New Year at Hyatt Centric MG Road Bangalore

    Looking for an unforgettable New Year’s Eve party? Hyatt Centric MG Road Bangalore has a lavish spread of unlimited food and drinks at The Bengaluru Brasserie and party all night at the Liquid Lounge Bar with DJ Chris, Electro Smashers, alongside live music.

    The rooftop of The Bengaluru Brasserie offers a perfect spot to eat, drink and have a merry time with friends and family at the restaurant that is perched at the poolside. The open-air Liquid Lounge Bar gives you a taste of the famous Bengaluru nightlife as you party your way into the new decade.

    When: 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:00 PM onward

    Venue: The Bengaluru Brasserie and Liquid Lounge Bar


    New Year Celebration at Smaaash, Phoenix Market City Mall, Whitefield with DJ Millennium Jeff

    Celebrate Bollywood & hip-hop style with DJ Millennium Jeff this New Year’s Eve at Smaaash. Package includes unlimited food, beverages, games and music.


    Social in association with Johnnie Walker present: Year B(#end)ER 2019 at all 4 outlets in Bangalore

    This event is everything an epic NYE party should be: One with the food and drinks worthy of becoming your final meal of the year (have you ever tried the Black Label Butter Chicken? and, the best music to become the soundtrack to the epic conclusion to the decade.

    So come on over and turn your decade’s worth of bad decisions into fun new ones at their ultimate YEAR B[END]ER 2019 party.

    Date: 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:00 pm onwards

    Venue: Sarjapur Social | Church Street Social | Whitefield Social | Koramangala Social


    Final Countdown at Four Seasons Hotel Bengaluru at Embassy ONE Restaurants

    Year-end jubilations will make for great ushering of the New Year and CUR8 has planned a New Year’s party with a DJ, music, great food and a bustling team of bartenders mixing up delicious cocktails. 2020 can be welcomed with Four Seasons flair.

    Date: Tuesday, December 31, 2019

    Time: 07:00pm – 01:00am

    Venue: CUR8


    'Beyond 2020' at 1Q1 Kitchen & Bar, Infantry Road, Central Bengaluru

    Bring in the New Year at the most extravagant affair in town. Presenting ‘Beyond 2020’ at 1Q1 Kitchen & Bar on 31st December at 09:00 PM onwards! The entire space will be decked and decorated with special lighting and the stage will be set up with mind-blowing LED Panels showcasing some of the best visuals throughout the night.


    DJ Joy will create the ultimate vibe playing a set of Dance and Commercial to start the year off like never before. Come, celebrate as we welcome 2020 over decadent cocktails, great Asian cuisine and experience so unique that you are sure to create new memories that will never fade.

    Date: Tuesday, 31st December 2019

    Time: 09:00 PM onwards


    Check Out two Exciting New Year 2020 Party Deals at Raahi, St. Marks Road, Central Bengaluru

    Short on party ideas for New Year Eve 2020? No need to worry as Raahi has got you covered this holiday season. Known for its fabulous ambience and delectable menu, this popular spot is all set to woo you with its exciting holiday celebration packages. Spend some quality time with your family at Raahi and make this jolly season even more memorable.

    Deals:

    New Year's Eve Dinner: Unlimited Food, Unlimited IMFL for Couple

    New Year's Eve Dinner: Unlimited Food, Unlimited IMFL for Single Entry


    New Year’s at Hard Rock Cafe, St. Marks Road, Central Bengaluru

    If there is one thing that Hard Rock Cafe has managed to do it is - maintain its quality over the years. The service is always pretty good – gig night or not and the food and drink quite a top-notch. They treat their meats with love and maybe that is why I love them all the more. This New Year, check out their special deal on offer.


    A Dazzling New Year’s Bash at Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield

    Ring in the New Year with a celebratory gala dinner at Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield as they present enticing offers for you and your loved ones. Be spoilt for choice as you celebrate the beginning of a new year while you toast goodbye to the old one. Join in for an unforgettable and super remarkable start to the year 2020!

    The Glamorous Gala Dinner at Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield

    Raise your glasses high while counting down to the stroke of midnight at the most glamorous gala dinner in Bengaluru. Rejoice as you culminate the year at Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield while the DJ spins the latest Bollywood hits so you can dance your way into the New Year. Offering guests a feast for the eyes and taste buds, the indulgent and delicious buffet spread will be accompanied with an unlimited pour of premium liquor.

    Venue: Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield

    Date: Tuesday, 31st December 2019

    Time: 07:00 PM onwards

    Ring in 2020 on a Delicious Note at Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield

    Bid adieu to 2019 and toast to the prosperous New Year as Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield hosts a delectable brunch on the first day of 2020. Join in the festivities at M Cafe for a special buffet brunch like no other with a never-ending feast of scrumptious goodies & treats accompanied by live entertainment and fun. The talented culinarians at the property are determined to take you on a gastronomical sojourn with signature dishes like Whole Roasted Pork, Roasted Turkey with live counters of Pasta, Bruschetta, Western Barbeque. Satiate your sweet cravings with an array of desserts including Flambe Plum Pudding along with an array of Indian desserts. Be spoilt for choice as Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield has it all to kick-start your New Year on a splendid note.

    Venue: M Cafe, Bengaluru Marriott Hotel Whitefield

    Date: Wednesday, 1st January 2020

    Time: 01:00 PM to 04:00 PM
