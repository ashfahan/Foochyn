    Critic Rating

    Phantom Speaks Secret Restaurant Reviewer

    4.2/5

    It is the one of its kind of glacial stone parlour chain across the country, where real desserts are fused with the ice creams to get the taste of the real deal! To go with the whole concept of the flower power era, right from their interiors to uniforms to names of these whacky creations scream boho chic. Their outlets also offer the best-stoned ice creams, bubble waffles, thick shakes, ice cream cakes along with your favourite classic beverages like tea, coffee, and hot chocolate. All ice creams have 15 per cent cream, making them richer and creamier in flavour. They serve wacky ice creams and boozy milkshakes which should not be missed. For the chocolate fans, ‘Brownie Blast’ - a double scoop dark chocolate ganache ice-cream stoned with in-house special brownie and topped with a secret caramel sauce. The ‘Skippy Banana’, ‘Chikki Chikki Bang Bang’, ‘Charlie and the Chocolate Factory’, ‘Bounty Hunter’, ‘Good ol' Hot Choc Fudge’, ‘Classic Banana Split’ are some of the must-haves.
