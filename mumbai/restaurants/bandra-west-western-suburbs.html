    About Bandra West, Western Suburbs, Mumbai

    Restaurant deals and offers in Bandra West, Western Suburbs


    Bandra West is a hip trendy locality where you can sit facing the old bungalows and sip a cup of coffee watching the fashionable crowd moving around you. This area is an attractive compilation of earthy microbreweries, hip bars and spectacularly designed restaurants ideal for a special occasion. Make Bandra West your destination this Day by choosing from its vast selection an idyllic good-looking restaurant. If its dim sums you want then go to the best Chinese restaurant in town - ‘Hakkasan’. The place is stylish, elegant and totally glam. ‘Ming Yang’ at Taj Lands End is another date-worthy opportunity for Chinese food. Now if your love craves Modern Indian fare then head for ‘JLWA’ on Linking Road. Traditional North Indian cuisine at its best can be found at ‘Masala Bay’ at Taj Lands End. Woo your palate with great European food at ‘Indigo Delicatessen’ or ‘Salt Water Café’.
