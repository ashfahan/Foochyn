    Critic Rating

    Ruth DSouza Journalist | Features writer | Wordsmith | Restaurant & Travel Blogger

    3.8/5

    Communiti is a quaint little space – with a really long spelling to its name, but one which catches your eye. Step in and the first thing you see is a long community sized table to accommodate a really large group or one that can be broken down into smaller pockets that you can occupy. There is a first floor as well. There are currently 7 beers on tap and a sampler will have you sorted in terms of making a choice. The basics of the 'Wit' and 'Hefeweizen' are good. Then, you also have the 'Irish Ale', a good stout and Belgian Wheat. We were lucky to get the last of the 'Honey Ale' and if you spot that again, do give it a whirl. The menu has a good choice when it comes to the nibbles. We loved the 'Pork Belly' and the 'Devilled Egg'. The 'Beef Chimmichuri' is also a great option. If you like your Mediterranean dips, then the 'Lebanese Platter' is one to go for. The 'Paprika BBQ Wings' is a nice option as is the 'Home made Nachos with Chicken Chunks'. For those of you who need the spice levels skyrocketing with your beers, the fiery hot chicken is a good one to consider. There are of course the substantial burgers – the 'Scrambled Cottage Cheese Burger' and the 'Juicy Chicken Burger' are good choices. Take look at the range of desserts at the display and make your pick. There are some great ones to choose from.

    About Communiti, Residency Road, Central Bengaluru


    When all the company you need are beer and chicken wings then the best place for you to visit would be your favourite microbrewery. There are tons of microbreweries in Bengaluru and new ones are opening up every single day, multiplying your choices for fresh haunts. Like most cafes and bars adapting the community table culture to encourage socializing, few microbreweries in the city are taking the same route as well, specifically Communiti. This new kid on the block is creating buzz on Bengalurus drinking scene by introducing large community tables seating style and sweetening the deal with an amazing beer and grub selection. Communiti is tucked in a sprawling two-story Brigade Solitaire Building on Residency Road in Bengaluru and attracts the eye with a vibrant faade.

    If you call yourself a connoisseur of beer then you have come to the right place. The microbrewery keeps as many as 7 beers on tap at all times and offers a sampler to help you make a clear decision. They produce a vast number of craft beers, churned in-house that are quite good. From a wonderful Irish ale to a pretty decent Belgian Wheat, whatever they put out is delicious. Dont miss out on their Honey Ale, its amazing. Match the brew with some of the choicest nibbles off the bars eclectic food menu. Once your appetite is whetted, why not dig in further with more fulfilling choices. You will be spoilt for options. Tuck into, bunny chow chicken, pasta arrabiata, tenderloin steak sizzler, farmhouse pizza, beef patty burger and country pork ribs.

    Find room for another pint or finish with a mouth-watering carrot cake. Communiti is one of the few bars in Bengaluru that is pet friendly. Bring your canine friend next time you go partying. Tap into their wi-fi to pass the time or to work. Prices are quite reasonable, although a tad higher than one expects. Decorated in a warm, homely manner, Communiti comes across differently than its stylish, modern peers. Exposed brick walls, variety of comfortable seating styles, log accent walls, soft dim lighting and vast space, lends the microbrewery an inviting appeal. The dcor encourages you to linger for hours, chatting and drinking with your gang. Sit in their outdoor section on pleasant evenings.

    This is a casual dining restaurant which is located on 67 & 68 Brigade Solitaire Building, Residency Road, in Bangalore. You get outstanding European food here. It is open from 11:30 AM to 1 A.M at night. A meal for two costs roughly INR 1200. This quaint little place catches your eye with its interesting décor. If you come in a big group you can take the long community sized table. They also have a sitting on first floor. The sitting is comfortable. The place in fact has been featured in City’s Favorite. This microbrewery serves the finest beers. They have 7 beers on tap along with a sampler. So, you are basically sorted as far as making a choice is concerned. You can get your pets along as this eating joint is pet friendly. Outdoor sitting is also available so that guests can enjoy the lovely weather when they want.

    Some of the signature dishes of Communiti are Homemade Nachos with Chicken chunks, Pork Belly, Devilled Egg, Beef Chimmichuri, Paprika BBQ Wings, Beef Chiimichuri, Scrambled Cottage Cheese Burger, Chilli Texan Fries, Roasted Garlic and Chicken Broth, Juicy Chicken Burger, etc. The deserts served here are very nice. You can choose any as per your taste. You must try the Carrot Cake. Match screening is arranged regularly to entertain the guests. They have take –away facility for those who do not wish to dine here or who do not get the table.

    There is wheelchair access available at Communiti in case there the guests find any problem getting inside. The food is prepared with proper technique and precision. The good thing about this place is that you can get the food customized in accordance to your taste. Service staff is quick, courteous and very professional in their approach. Book your table at Communiti well in advance as you might not get a table without prior reservation. You can book online or get table booking on phone. Payment is accepted both in cash and by card. Valet parking facility is available for ease of the guests
