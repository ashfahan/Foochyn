    About Cream Centre, Nungambakkam, Chennai


    If you are craving for some amazing multi cuisine food then Cream Centre located on College Road in Chennai is a wonderful joint to visit with friends and family. Order the Delhi Tawa Paratha Meal which is a mlange of superb flavors. Dishes such as Dum Birayani & rice, Cream Centre Vegetable Biryani, Italian Basil Tomato Spaghetti, Pasta- In Pasta, Paneer Paratha, Methi Aloo Paratha, Chana Bhatura are a favorite among guests. In desserts you can order Malai Kulfi and Sizzling Brownie. The flavors are authentic and you will not miss presence of non- vegetarian food at all.

    This place is simply ideal for a casual dining experience. The pretty wall papers add to appeal of this place. The lighting is classy and table setting is very good. As the dishes are prepared using the best available ingredients, the quality which you get is simply awesome. Service is just up to the mark and you will actually have no reason to complain once you enter Cream Centre with your near and dear ones.

    The presentation of food at Cream Center makes is just impeccable. The furniture, dcor everything is understated. There is a lot of natural light which make this place feel fresh and nice. Many people like to visit here with their family as the food prices are affordable. Service staff is amicable and knowledgeable. They fully know how to please the guests.

    Any person who visits the Cream Center comes back happy and satisfied. This is basically a chain restaurant with first center located in Marine Lines in Mumbai. This is a huge reason for its well established client base as people know that they will get quality food here. Always book your table in advance as this place is generally quite jam packed and you might be refused a seat during rush hours. So, for a superb gastronomical experience make a beeline to Cream centre which is equally popular among youth and the elderly population. Book online or make a call to confirm your table reservation.
