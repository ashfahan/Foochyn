    About Velachery, Chennai, Chennai

    Valentine's Day Restaurant deals and offers in Velachery, Chennai


    Velachery is an ultra-cool slice of Chennai city brimming with trendy restaurants and funky eateries that will serve you beautifully this Valentine’s Day. Don those killer heels and make a reservation for two at any of Velachery’s hip global restaurants. You may feel like some spicy curry on this special day. If yes, then bag a table at ‘Pind’ or ‘Punjab Grill’ to savor delectable North Indian curries. ‘Andhikkadai’ and ‘Appa Sutta Dosai’ is where you can mop up tangy sambhar with airy idlis. Relish awesome Pan Asian food from different corners of Asia at ‘Orange Wok’ or ‘Mamagoto’. If your taste run towards Chinese then there is nothing better than ‘Mainland China’. When it comes to European food, ‘Kobe Sizzlers’ takes the main stage at Velachery. Sit down in comfort and feast on Velachery’s outstanding gastronomy on your special day.

    VELACHERY CHENNAI WORLD CUP

    Falling on the southern side of Chennai, Velachery is a rapidly growing residential and IT hubs in the past decade and is the perfect blend of old and new Chennai. The fine dining landscape of Velachery creates quite a stir amongst the locals and is decorated with a vast number of restaurants and bars of different statures. For upper crust, The Westin Hotel is an obvious playground, fitted with various restaurants and bars to dazzle your mind, and throughout Cricket World Cup 2019 these restaurants are offering fantastic discounts to it’s cricket fanatic patrons and also the chance to watch the match on big screen.

    For a blend of romance and cricket, choose Sunset Grill as your destination. This charming fine dining restaurant at Westin pampers your palate with exotic Middle Eastern food and entertain you by putting up live streaming of the matches throughout world cup. Feast on a buffet 24X7 at Seasonal Tastes, Westin’s elegant all-day dining restaurant. For Velachery natives with hectic schedule, this restaurant acts as a wonderful pit stop. Imbibe on a few boozy drinks while you hoot for your team of blue at Willows, a sophisticated watering hole tucked at Westin in Velachery. Live screening of matches and drool-worthy discounts keeps the world cup season interesting.
