    About 3B's, Country Inn & Suites By Carlson, Ghaziabad


    To make a special occasion even more special, put emphasis on the ambience. A good striking ambience goes a long way into transforming any ordinary evening. For those living around Ghaziabad, your search for an amazing ambient restaurant has come to an end. Trust us and book yourself a table at ‘3B’s’, an elegant multi-cuisine fine dining restaurant ideal to give any ordinary meal an added oomph. Whatever the occasion may be, 3B’s will deliver. Be it your mother’s birthday or a quiet business dinner with a colleague, an elegant table at this refined restaurant will do justice to the occasion. Ride the elevator to the 1st Floor, Country Inn & Suites by Carlson, Sahibabad, Ghaziabad, where the restaurant is located. The restaurant fills up quickly, book a table ahead.

    3B’s caters to a vast clientele pouring in and out of the hotel regularly so the menu you see here is eclectic. The classic trio of Indian food scene - North Indian, Continental & Italian – amalgamates on the menu, creating a fantastic selection of dishes to impress every kind of diner. Country Inn & Suites is known to be a vegetarian hotel chain so naturally, 3B’s is purely vegetarian as well. Try one of 3B’s hand crafted cocktails. Pomerioshka is our special favorite poison. Match it with an appetizer of your choice. We recommend reshmi paneer tikka, crispy vegetable, Mexican cottage cheese & jalapeno fingers, zafrani bharwan chaap, homemade honey chilli potato, falafel & pita bread sandwiches and tandoori khumb achari. Fill yourself on, zafrani bharwan chaap sizzler, Mediterranean pizza, angoori kofta, exotic vegetables in black pepper sauce, eggplant zucchini timbale and banarasi dum aloo.

    Indulge your sweet tooth with a plethora of desserts like, chocolate fondue, kala jamun with vanilla ice cream, ultimate brownie sizzler, darsan with ice-cream and flambe American wild berry/banana. They host live music nights and sports screenings occasionally. Entrance to 3B’s is quite striking. A pair of tribal guards stands flanking the doorway, which opens into a spacious elegant room overlooking the pool. Dining room is furnished with plush button-back couches and cozy tables for smaller groups. Futuristic chandelier overhead adds that glow to the room. Try to snag a table next to the huge window to get an unbarred view of the soothing pool. Décor is modern, smart and lounge-y.
