    Located in multiple places, Chaayos is a popular cafe and much-loved for serving a variety of tea options and quick snacks. This modern version of a tea stall is clean and hygienic and great if you are craving for a hot cup of tea. The cosy corners are seamless for spending quality time with your loved ones over a delightful indulgence into an assortment of snacks and tea. Sabudana Tikki, Keema Pav, Matar Kulcha are some of the snacks that go well with tea and are flavoursome too.

    About Chaayos, DLF Cyber City, Gurgaon

    Britishers have given India a lot of things while their rule but appreciation for a cup of tea tops them all. It was during their rule in India that they discovered and encouraged tea plantations, which today has gained fame all over the world. Now tea has become a household name. Every person living in India rushes for their 4’o clock tea break to revive themselves. It was only recently that tea parlors have popped up all over Delhi NCR giving stiff competition to coffee houses. We owe that to ‘Chaayos’, a cozy welcoming tea parlor perfect for your every tea craving. This popular chain of tea parlors take deep pride in their awesome tea selection and has opened several branches across Gurgaon. DLF Cyber City houses multiple outlets of Chaayos in various corners of this popular locality.
