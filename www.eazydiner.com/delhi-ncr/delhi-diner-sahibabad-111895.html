    About Delhi Diner, Pacific Mall, Ghaziabad


    Those more centrally located in Delhi get to enjoy the exciting side of this wonderful city but those living on the side, miss out on loads of fun happenings. Gastronomy is a major force in the city driven by banks of trendy restaurants and hip new pubs surfacing in all the fancy posh locales of Delhi NCR. Places like Ghaziabad that are so far off crave for its own version of fabulous gastronomy but in recent years have seen an increase in its dining scene by the arrival of fancy luxurious hotels. Pacific Mall is a well-loved property in Ghaziabad and is home to some delightful restaurants. Its all-day dining coffee shop ‘Delhi Diner’ is ideally suited to families and executives looking for some quiet place to dine at or to have an important discussion. It’s located at Clarks Inn, Pacific Mall, Sahibabad, Ghaziabad.

    If you are part of a large group, choosing to dine at Delhi Diner would work in your favour. This all-day dining restaurant keeps a varied global menu that covers multiple cuisines in one place. For a group with diverse palates, their eclectic menu turns out to be a blessing. A pleasing combination of North Indian, Chinese, Italian and Continental, woos your palate. They back it up with a superb bar selection to accentuate the experience. If not breakfast, begin your meal at Delhi Diner with an array of appetizers. Save your appetite for the main course that entails dishes like, pan fried fish in herb butter, mutton rogan josh, hot & sour vegetables, nargisi malai kofta, Delhi Diner chicken burger, chilli chicken with hot garlic sauce and pindi choley.

    Menu is comforting and holds plenty of choices for the patrons. Best way to finish off the meal would be with a rasgulla, the classic Bengali sweet. The restaurant hosts live sports screening during seasons. Interiors are typical of a Delhi hotel, elegant and modern. Décor has a timeless quality to it. While there is nothing imaginative or outstanding about the interiors, Delhi Diner still comes across as refined and semi-formal. Try to grab the table next to the window so that you can admire the view of the mall with your meal. Delhi Diner is a good place to rest a while or kill time over snacks in the mall. Prices are a tad hefty, keep that in mind.
