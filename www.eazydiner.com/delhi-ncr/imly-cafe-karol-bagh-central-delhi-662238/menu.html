    INSIDER TIPS

    • The eatery is a heaven for those guilty of overdosing on gol gappas, or are known to hunt out the best chaat places in the city
    • One of the best features of this restaurant is its ambience as some are designed like a train or like the London Bus
    • Favourites on their menu are the 'Rice The Imly Way', 'Dahi Golgappe Chaat', 'the classic Banta Shikanji', 'Gunpowder Fried Idli', 'Corn Nachos Chaat', 'Cheese Kurkuri' and of course, 'jalebi'

    Phantom Speaks

    3.9 /5

    Imly brings together the street food of the entire country. Lucknow, Amritsar, Surat, Chennai, Varanasi, Delhi and Mumbai are just some of the cities represented on this all-vegetarian map. Imly focuses on serving hygienic street food retaining the taste of how one would like to savour the taste from the street vendors. The 'corn and cheese tandoori momo', 'golgappa', 'cheese pav bhaji' and 'vada pav' are some of the must-haves. The space is big enough for big families with kids and can accommodate groups of people. If you’re planning that dinner with your family since a while, must head here. The place gets crowded during the evenings and weekends, hence, prior reservations are a must.
