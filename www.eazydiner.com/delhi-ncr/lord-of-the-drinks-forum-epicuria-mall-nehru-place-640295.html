    Phantom Speaks Secret Restaurant Reviewer

    4.5/5

    This multi-cuisine bar and pub is in Nehru Place, New Delhi. Its attractive ambience makes it apt to party with your friends or colleagues. The place is huge with comfortable seating areas. Lord of the Drinks specializes in Indian, Italian, Chinese and European cuisine. Whatever you may order, begin your culinary journey here by ordering their cocktails like ‘Vodka Tini’, ‘Cosmopolitan’, ‘Spicy Love’, or ‘Mojito’. You must try their mouth-watering starters like ‘Barbequed Dhokla Chaat’, ‘Crispy Corn Bhel’, ‘Honey Chilli Fries’, ‘Salt & Pepper Chicken’ or ‘Chicken Bangkok Pao’. On the other hand, if you love eating Dim Sums, you must try ‘Chicken Thai Herb Dim Sums’, ‘Chicken Gyozas’ or ‘Mix Veg Dumplings’. Or you can even try their lip-smacking tandoori starters like ‘Tandoori Soya Chaap’, ‘Paneer Tikka’, or ‘Kalonji Mutton Kebabs’. Slurp on a variety of domestic & imported beer, along with vodka and whiskies with your meal here. If you love Italian cuisine, then you must try their freshly baked pizzas like ‘Lords of Veggies’, ‘Classic Pepperoni’ or a ‘Roast Chicken Pizza’, for the main course. You must also try their delectable pastas like ‘Alfredo Penne’, ‘Aglio Olio Pasta’ or ‘Spaghetti Carbonara’, if you are a pasta lover. The place also serves scrumptious ‘Loaded Veg Burger’ or ‘Asian Chicken Burger’.

    About Lord Of The Drinks Forum, Epicuria Mall, Nehru Place


    If you want to enjoy authentic European, Chinese, North Indian, Italian in Delhi then Lord of the Drinks Forum which is a casual dining would be a preferred choice. The wonderful restaurant with rich and cozy ambiance is found in the Epicuria Food Mall, Nehru Place. Enjoy the serene ambiance of this place sitting in the air-conditioned restaurant. The stylish restaurant reverberates with live DJ and sets the mood of everyone and makes the evening very lively. The appealing display of lights underlines the striking vibes of this place. A meal for two costs approximately INR 1800. Payment can be made in cash, cards and digital payments are accepted here. The place is open from 11am to 12:45am

    The place is very hygienic and superb quality ingredients are used here. The highlight of this place are extraordinary Mocktails, load of options for cocktail lovers, Beer, Long Island Iced Tea, variety of Pizzas, Pasta and Dahi Kebab. Amazing North Indian preparations are made with a contemporary touch. You can try their Chicken Momos, Gaulati Kababs with the Parantha, Alfredo Pasta, Guava Chicken Salad, Honey Chilli Potato, Salt and Pepper Veg, Chilli Chicken, Chicken Dumplings, Butter Chicken with Butter Naan, The aroma of scrumptous food hovers in the air enticing your taste buds. Innovative dishes are frequently added to the main course. The desserts though limited like Kheer, Ice Cream Sundae, Tiramisu but are a must try.

    The courteous staff is super friendly and offer suggestions as what to order and how to pair it with the right choice of drinks. The waiters go out of the way to make you feel comfortable and create memorable moments for you. Its outstanding ambience, live screening of the sporting events, Separate smoking area, Live Music with a good play list, Nightlife and a well equipped bar make Lord of the Drinks Forum a perfect venue where you can celebrate a special occasion with your family, friends and your loved one. To avoid last minute inconvenience table booking is recommended. So, get the table booked with us and avail great discounts and offers. A must visit place if you want to enjoy a relaxed evening.
