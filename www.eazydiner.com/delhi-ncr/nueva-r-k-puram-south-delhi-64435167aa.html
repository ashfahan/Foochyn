    Critic Rating

    Ruchira Hoon speaks Journalist, food blogger and a food consultant

    3.7/5

    It is the beautiful kitchen that draws you into the restaurant. But by then you’re quite taken in by the décor. Classy with beautiful modern touches, this is one restaurant that actually values your privacy, setting up tables far apart so that you cannot overhear the next tables conversations. The dishes have esoteric names, but it is the taste that does not quite live up to its mark. Next up was the 'Tea-Infused Chicken' which honestly sounded better on paper than how it tasted. Under seasoned and sharp on the tannins, this is one of the most missable things on the menu. The 'Duck Moile', however, was delicious. This spicy curry served with rice was comforting and rich. For dessert, we opted to eat ‘The Paintbox’ - which was more to do with art than to actually eat it. If you do eat it, the edginess of the lemon curd will hit you hard. The 'Flan' too has the same problem.

    About Nueva, R K Puram, South Delhi


    The dynamics of Delhi NCR’s gastronomic scene is changing as the palates of the diners are becoming more evolved. European food is finding it’s way deeper into the fold and is bringing with it a fresh exotic approach of those untapped regions, which the city is just waking up to. These newly dawned cuisines are innovative and bold in style, filling the gourmets with an excitement of exploring an untapped territory. Revolutionizing the dining scene lately is a new kid on the block called ‘Nueva’, where global cuisines are getting a modern makeover. This gorgeous looking restaurant is located on the Ground Floor, Sangam Courtyard, R K Puram, New Delhi, a node of stylish restaurants bursting with life. Nueva may be fairly new on the scene but has quickly piqued our interest with its air of originality.

    Nueva has come to be known for it’s innovative style in Western, Latin American and Peruvian cuisine. It’s fascination for South American food has bought on a sense of adventure amongst the local food community as they are rushing to book a table at this popular progressive restaurant. Food is definitely expensive but to save yourself a bundle, look into EazyDiner offers or Nueva coupons. Variety is immense, one trip won’t be nearly enough to explore the menu fully. Opt for a bottle of wine to pair the meal. Start with, mariscos a la plancha, chimichurri mushrooms, Navajo spiced calamari, Andean corn & beans salad, peruanito and tamalitos. Dig deeper with, flat bread & filo pizza, Brazilian barbeque grilled chicken, pollo asado, Nuestra paella, Brazilian braised fava beans, lobestor a la plancha, steak argentino and vegetables chilli lime.

    Sip a tall order of Pure Verde, a delicious cooler made with kiwi, cucumber, spicy jalapenos and lime remix straight to Peru. Indulge your sweet tooth with, two way quinoa pudding, parched, cinnamon tiramisu, the paint box, arroz con leche and soufflé de chocolate. Their seasonal menu changes every few months, keep a track of that. Food is beautifully styled and each dish is a work of art. Interiors of Nueva are straight out of a lifestyle magazine. Décor is the epitome of elegance with modern sleek lines attracting your eye instantly. Warm woody tones are used to create the space that lends a welcoming vibe to the room. Ambience is refined and serene at times. It’s a great place to bring someone special.
