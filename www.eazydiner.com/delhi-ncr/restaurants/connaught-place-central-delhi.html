    About Connaught Place (CP), Central Delhi, Delhi NCR

    Connaught Place is the heart of Delhi pulsating with history, power, culture and life. This complex node of historical buildings and patches of dense green foliage today, used to be a ridge covered with kikar trees and housed by jackals. Residents of Kashmere Gate and Civil Lines visited during the weekends for partridge hunting. This area was later developed by the government as a central business district and was named after Prince Arthur, 1st Duke of Connaught. Development of Connaught Place fell upon the shoulders of W.H Nicholls, the chief architect to the Government of India, but later the work was given to Robert Tor Russel who went on to design the complex as it is today. With time Connaught Place grew in shape and size welcoming the first cinema, restaurants and posh homeowners in its fold.

    The entire swirl of Connaught Place is divided into a circular cluster of buildings, opening into various wide lanes tipped by glittery showrooms, restaurants and commercial sector. Beautiful Georgian architecture arrests one’s eye with its high arches running parallel to the entire circle. A central park is erected at the heart of Connaught Place, cleverly disguising the famous Palika Bazar and then branching into a spread of stunning skyscrapers gleaming in the sun. Tucked into this melange of foliage and concrete is historical heritage buildings like Agrasen ki Baoli and more modern versions like National Philatelic Museum and The Imperial. Today Connaught Place is just not a centre of power and history but it has become a sought after destination for gastronomy and retail. Just hop in a cab or a metro and come experience the old world magic of Connaught Place.

    Over the years Connaught Place has become a major hub of restaurants and pubs. Its swanky belt of trendy dining spots targets the world of global gastronomy with an ease, housing some of the bigwigs of culinary circle of the country. On one hand the gastronomy of Connaught Place is old-fashioned and historical (Kwality, United Coffee House, Embassy Restaurant, Wenger’s) and on the other hand it showcases modern and innovative culinary talents (Farzi Café, Veda, Barbeque Nation, Jungle Jamboree, Garam Dharam, Tamra). Connaught Place is also the party central of the city featuring sensational pubs for you to taste the nightlife like, HotMess, Lord of the Drinks, Junkyard Café, The Vault Café and Aqua. There is something for everyone in CP.
