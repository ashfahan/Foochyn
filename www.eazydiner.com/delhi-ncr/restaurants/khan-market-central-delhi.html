    About Khan Market, Central Delhi, Delhi NCR

    The history of Khan Market in New Delhi is very colourful and interesting. The city dwellers know Khan Market to be one of the most expensive place to live and shop in the city which is absolutely true till date. The market was named in honour of Khan Abdul Jabbar Khan who helped the nation during the trying times of partition. The first traders who came to Delhi after the partition set up shops here and reside in few flats above the shops but slowly after a few decade they sold and moved on, converting the flats into another row of retail shops. As times moved on these humble shops took the face of glittery and posh residents and converted themselves into the sophisticated avatar of themselves. The double story Khan Market raised from its humble origins and entered into the posh world of fashion and gastronomy.

    Khan Market is heavily influenced by the surrounding localities of the city which includes government and privately owned residential complexes. Posh areas like Golf Links, Lodhi Estate, Shahjahan Road, Pandara Road, Rabindra Nagar and Sujan Singh Park are the perfect companion to the beauty of Khan Market. The Market is fortunately located in the stunningly green pockets of the city and the surrounding lush sectors only accentuates the beauty of this area. Khan Market is very close the famous Lodhi Gardens which lends its sprawling green landscape as the ideal backdrop to the market. You would find international clothing brands, global grocery stores with imported produce, chic coffee shops, stylish restaurants and glitzy jewellery stores forming the face of the market. The modern showrooms falls into a neat row with fashionable imaginative windows displaying their fare to their full glory.

    Food wise Khan Market has a lot to offer. Lovely Pan Asian food gets a stunning platform at Mamagoto while exotic Mexican fare is displayed at the best advantage at La Bodega. For those who like little bit of everything under one roof gets to savour beautiful food at Smoke House Deli and Latitude 28. Grab a juicy red hot American burger at Smokey's BBQ And Grill and if you want to indulge in a drinking binge with friends then we suggest Harry's and Boombox Caf for you. Throw in a bit of adventure by bringing Afro-Portuguese cuisine in the mix at Barcelos. Khan Market is exceptional and unique place so visit and explore its hidden surprises.
