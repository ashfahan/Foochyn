    Phantom Speaks

    4.3 /5

    Toniq is an amazing dry bar-cafe. They have interesting interiors and seating space; the food is mouthwatering, and the drinks are refreshing. Also, they serve food in nice takeaway tin boxes. One must try their 'Paneer Tikka Fries' and 'Chicken Tikka Pizza'. This place serves delicious fries which are crinkle cut and super crispy. This restaurant even has outdoor seating, with a glass wall.
