    • 10 Best Places to Catch ICC Cricket World Cup 2019 in Chandigarh

    Adding zing to the cricketing season are discounts and deals!

    18 Jun, 2019 by Jagmeeta Thind Joy

    With the summer heat rising, it is time to make the best of indoor spaces. The ICC Cricket World Cup 2019 could not have been better timed. It is time to put on your jerseys, sit back and cool off with some refreshing drinks and meals. Catch all the cricketing action at venues across town. Huge screens are up and there are special meals and deals too. Here are the top 10 recommendations in Chandigarh.


    Boathouse, Elante Mall, Chandigarh

    The best part about this nautical themed microbrewery is the large space and the cool brews on offer. Make the most of the multi-cuisine spread on offer especially their Orange and Basil Ice Tea which goes well with the Boatie Long Straight Pizzas. If you are looking to try something unique, do order the Bibimbap, a Korean rice bowl dish.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 20% off on food & soft beverage and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    Sultan, Sector 7, Chandigarh

    Sultan, a fine-dining restaurant, has a very good selection of soups, shorbas, starters, platters, curries, breads and even a desi take on pizzas. If you are looking for a group outing, Sultan pleases all palates. Do try the shareable platters.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    Citrus Cafe, Lemon Tree Hotel, Chandigarh

    This vibrant eatery is a good place to head to if you are looking for a buffet meal. The club sandwiches are our favourite and there are specials on offer even past dinner time. While you cheer on your team, do relish their global cuisine offerings. There is also an all-time favourite section on the menu as well.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 1+1 on Food Item (A La Carte Menu Only) and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    The Reef, Sector 7, Chandigarh

    When summer temperatures soar high, we all crave for coolers and fresh fare. Inspired by the high seas, The Reef offers cool respite and sumptuous fare. A good place to start is the Watermelon and Feta Cheese Salad along with the Reefs Grilled Panini Sandwich. When watching a match, we love to pair our drinks with finger food. The Reef offers plenty of options including loaded Nachos. The Steamed Baos are another speciality.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 25% off on food & soft beverage and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    Xtreme Sports Bar & Grill, Sector 22, Chandigarh

    When it comes to places with an unmistakable fun vibe especially during sporting events, Xtreme Sports Bar & Grill is a frontrunner. Apart from the pocket-friendly prices, Xtreme offers a good line-up of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. They have a special world cup menu on offer and live screening of the matches. A good place to cheer for India with your gang of friends.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 20% off on food & soft beverage and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    Social, Sector 7

    A popular place for community events and foodie specials, Social in Sector 7 is hosting specials all through the cricketing season. There is a whole new line-up of thalis and an interesting breakfast offer at just Rs 199, every day between 9 am to 12 noon.


    Egg Specials

    The restaurant has also joined hands with Uber to make match days even better! Diners get a 10% off their ride to Social as well one free shot at the bar.

    Book now! Click here


    Pipe and Barrel, Sector 9, Chandigarh

    This is a place that gets the party going. The lounge-like seating is perfect for watching the match with friends.


    Chilli Chicken

    The menu brings in a multi-cuisine spread and there is plenty of tandoori snacks to munch on with beer. There are happy hours and deals on offer.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 25% off on food & soft beverage and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    BoomBox Cafe, Sector 9, Chandigarh

    While BoomBox Cafe is very popular for live music gigs, it is quite the place to chill post a work day. If you are looking to watch the cricketing action and dine with friends, head over to the Boombox cafe.


    Grilled Chicken

    Their Chicken Steak is well done and the array of sides is good. You could also try the Keema Pao that is quite a speciality here.

    Book now! Click here


    Rebelle, Sector 7, Chandigarh

    This newly opened restaurant and lounge has a nice arrangement for watching the match. There is a huge screen that is visible from all angles. There is lounge seating as well. The menu hopscotches across cuisines but it is a good mix. Post matches they even host after parties with the DJ. Do try the tangy Lemon Chilli Prawns and the Biryani too.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 10% off on food between 8 pm and 11:30 pm and 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here


    Playground, Elante Mall, Chandigarh Industrial Area

    This cafe-style restaurant is also making the most of the world cup fever. Apart from screening the live match, the menu offers an interesting mix of food.


    Nachos Bowl

    The Nachos bowl is a must-try here. For those who like their meats, the tikka and kebabs platter pairs well with beer. The Bhuna Paneer Bao is perfect for those who like paneer. There are daily alcoholic promotions as well.

    World Cup Special Deal @ EazyDiner: 30% discount on food & beverage (for Axis Bank & Citibank cardholders only)

    Book now! Click here
