    • 12 Best Restaurants In Delhi-NCR To Dine At This Navratri

    Check out our list of restaurants you should not miss, satiating the vegetarian in you!

    18 Mar, 2018 by Editorial Team

    This Navratri season, restaurants across Delhi-NCR are coming up with innovative dishes and working hard to make the utmost devotees, also, wanting to eat out in their holiest of days. Amidst stark competition, restaurants are up for a game by experimenting to attract and retain customers. The dining destinations in Delhi-NCR will serve freshly prepared vegetarian cuisines during these days.

    Check out our list of restaurants you should not miss, satiating the vegetarian in you.

    SodaBottleOpenerWala, all across Delhi NCR

    This place is replete with quirky décor elements strongly reminiscent of Parsi homes, albeit a bit overstated. Their delectable dishes including, Vrat Ke Chawal, Kuttu Ke Atte Ki Poori and Sabudana Papad, are served with all combos you order.  The Kera Na Cutlet, Khattu Meethu Kaddu and many more dishes, will leave you spoilt for choices this fasting season.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 26th March 2018

    Where: All across Delhi NCR

    Deal: 1+1 on Special Navratri Thali

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table @ Cyber Hub

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table @ Khan Market

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table @ DLF Mall of India, Noida


    The Ancient Barbeque, Sohna Road, Gurgaon

    It is known for its variety of buffet spread and 'Live on Table' grill. The Chef at The Ancient Barbeque has carefully picked out the best to put together a special fast menu. The list comprises of Paneer Malai Tikka, Tawa Fruit Chaat, Kache Kele Ke Pakore, Dahi Ke Kebab, Aloo and Shakarkandi Ki Chaat, Lauki Aloo Jeera to finger licking Paneer Makhana Aur Kaju ka Sangam offered with Samak Ke Chawal and Kuttu And Singare Ki Poori are the attractions to the fasting platter.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Good Earth City Centre, Sector 50, Sohna Road, Gurgaon

    Deal: Special Navratri Thali for lunch @ INR 399 AI per person | Dinner @ INR 499 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table
     

    Indian Grill Room, Golf Course Road, Gurgaon

    This restaurant brings to you the choicest of dishes to choose from, specially crafted for the fasting souls. You can order for healthy options like Fruit Salad, Paneer Ke Pakode, Samak Ki Chawal, Kuttu Ki Poori, Kherey Ka Raita, Aloo Ki Sabji and Sabudana Kheer.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: 315, Suncity Business Tower, Sector 54, Golf Course Road, Gurgaon

    Deal: Special Navratri Thali for lunch and dinner @ INR 475 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table


    Edesia, Crowne Plaza, Okhla

    Edesia is popular for their all-day buffet spread is all set with innovative ideas to treat you during this fasting season. They are offering a special set menu with appetizing Navratri food which is hard to find. If you are also craving for some authentic Navratri grub, head over!

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Crowne Plaza, Community Centre, Okhla Phase 1, New Delhi

    Deal: Special Navratri Thali @ INR 899 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table
     

    Singh Sahib, Eros Hotel, Nehru Place

    Situated in the fancy Eros Hotel, the Navratri menu at Singh Sahib lives up to its expectations. The place is serving a Navratra Thali. The food includes delicious fast chows like Flavoured Badam Kesar Milk as a welcome drink, Sabudana Papad, Kuttu Ki Poori, Singhara Ke Pakode, Fruit Salad, Zeera Aloo, Samak Ke Chawal, Khera Raita, Gud Ki Gajak, Shahi Paneer and Sitaphal Ki Sabji. Add a perfect end to your meal with the Makhane Ki Kheer.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Eros Hotel, American Plaza, Nehru Place, New Delhi

    Deal: Special Navratri thali @ INR 1199 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table


    Zabardast Indian Kitchen, Connaught Place (CP), Central Delhi

    Celebrate this Navratra at Zabardast Indian Kitchen with their special likes of Paneer Subji, Dry Aloo, Kuttu ki Poori, Sabudana Papad, Samak Ke Chawal, Raita and Sabudana Kheer, the perfect meal if you are looking to have a simple and comforting treat.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: E-13/29, Ground Floor, Middle Circle, Connaught Place, New Delhi

    Deal: Special Navratri thali @ INR 427 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table

     
    Surahi By The Rajkamal, Connaught Place (CP), Central Delhi

    Surahi is located in the heart of the capital city - Connaught Place. This restaurant is all set to welcome its guests with a special delectable set menu for the all of you fasting out there. The Navratri Thali will include dishes such as Kuttu Ki Poori, Sabudana Papad, Kuttu Ki Aate Ke Pakoda, Samak Ki Chawal, Aloo Raita and Sabudana Kheer.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: M-58 & 59, Connaught Place, New Delhi

    Deal: Navratra thali @ INR 561 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table
     

    Rio New Delhi, Rajouri Garden

    This casual dining café offers one of the best lip-smacking munchies in Rajouri Garden and now they are all set to serve their Vrat Ka Khana, this fasting season. Feast on some of their mouth-watering dishes like Sabudana Khichdi, Singade Ke Pakode’, Sabudana Wada, Sitafal Ki Sabji, Kuttu Ki Poori.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Unit A1, Ground Floor, J1/164, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi

    Deal:  Navratra Thali @ INR 344 AI per person

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table


    Capers Restaurant, Golden Tulip Vasundhara Hotel and Suites, New Delhi

    Their quirky and bright décor is very welcoming. Not only their interiors, but they bring quirkiness to their plating too. From the delicious starter, Sabudana Papad to the delectable main-course that includes Kuttu Ki Poori, Samak Ke Chawal, Kashiphal Masala to the Arbi Ki Shorbedaar Sabji and for the desserts Lauki Ka Halwa to the Makhane Ka Kheer, this fine dining experience is curated especially, to satisfy your cravings for all 9 days.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Golden Tulip Vasundhara Hotel & Suites, C 5, Sector 18, Vasundhara, Ghaziabad

    Deal: Navratri Thali @ INR 650 AI

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table
     

    Tatva, Country Inn & Suites By Carlson, Ghaziabad

    Tatva is often considered as one of the best options for vegetarian food in Delhi NCR. It is a pure vegetarian restaurant serving traditional North Indian delicacies and will not leave you disappointed as the Navratri Thali contains food items such as Sabudane Ki Tikki, Zaike Daar Aloo, Makhane Ki Kheer amongst others, tailor-made for you!

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: Country Inn & Suites by Carlson, Site 4, Sahibabad, Ghaziabad

    Deal: Navratri Thali @ INR 825 AI

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table


    Marine Drivve, Rajouri Garden, West Delhi

    Keeping in mind the dietary restrictions and traditional preparations, their Navratri menu has been designed to give it a new flavour, texture and taste. The menu at Hungry Marine Drivve will have fast-friendly variants with a contemporary touch.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: A-2, Vishal Enclave, Opposite Metro Pillar 412, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi

    Deal: Navratri Tiffin @ INR 369 AI

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table
     

    The Headquarter, Rajouri Garden, West Delhi

    While the restaurants are planning on how to get innovative ideas to welcome guests for the 9 day festival season, The Headquarter is shouting out loud by offering a 50% discount on its Food & Beverage. If you are not the ones that are not on a fast, then hurry up this is the right place to hang out with your peers and party this season.

    Valid: 18th March 2018 to 25th March 2018

    Where: 2nd Floor, C-8, Vishal Enclave, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi

    Deal: Enjoy 50% off on food & soft beverage

    Click Here! To Reserve A Table

    These special menus and lavish thalis are giving Navratri fasting a delicious edge like none other. Feast as you fast, this season, with these delectable Navratri delicacies. Happy Navratri 2018.
