    Amazing places to step in with your loved ones this Valentine's Day

    21 Jan, 2020 by Editorial Team
    PC: Sevilla, The Claridges, New Delhi


    Valentine’s Day is around the corner and we can already feel the vibes of the day. While most people find it cheesy or cliché, this day is all spreading love and romance. Started with Archies Cards and Dairy Milk Chocolate or other fancy Chocolates, it has now turned to Long Drives or Valentine’s Day Lunch/Dinner Dates with your better half. Preparing food for someone you love is beautiful, but if anyone doesn’t know how to cook or do not want to cook, the following restaurants will save your life where you can take your Valentine to make a perfect beginning for a love-filled year ahead.


    QLA, Mehrauli, South Delhi

    Located in the peaceful and plunged into the shadow of the Qutab Minar, yet happening Mehrauli, Qla has luxurious contemporary interiors, that will give you a modern and vintage mix kind of feel. Whether it is a pleasant luncheon with your partner or a candle-lit dinner, Qla is a great place to spend your Valentine's day at.

    Where: The Kila, Seven Style Mile, Kalkadas Marg, Mehrauli, New Delhi

    Nearest Metro Station: Qutab Minar

    Cost for two: Rs 3500

    Rating: 4.5 

    Timings: 12:00 PM to 12:30 AM 

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Sevilla, The Claridges, New Delhi

    If you and your partner are looking for a quieter place where all the focus has to be just the two of you, then Sevilla at The Claridges is the best restaurant to be at, on Valentine's Day. Beautiful tent-like seating arrangements and lamp posts filling up the place with a romantic glow - what else would one want for a romantic candlelight dinner in Delhi? A great selection of champagnes and Spanish food will definitely impress your date and make the evening memorable. 

    Where: The Claridges, Aurangzeb Road, New Delhi

    Cost for two: Rs 3500

    Rating: 4.3

    Timings: 07:00 PM to 11:45 PM 

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Nukkad SDA, South Delhi

    If you are looking for the top places to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Delhi that are neither too expensive and nor out of reach, then this cafe in SDA market is the best place to be. The warm and cosy ambience makes it easier for anyone to have a romantic date.

    Where: C 23 - 24, SDA Market, New Delhi

    Cost for two: Rs 1200

    Rating: 4.2

    Timings: 11:30 AM to 01:00 AM

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    BOA VILLAGE, Civil Lines, North Delhi

    Located in Civil Lines, Boa Village is one of the best restaurants for Valentine’s Day in Delhi. With an indoor, outdoor, and a seating within a gorgeous glass house, this fine dining restaurant offers sweet experiences along with friendly hospitality. This is the ideal place to treat your taste buds and pamper your partner if you do not wish to splurge. This is the best restaurant in Delhi for couples, for Valentine’s Day.

    Where: 13, Alipur Road, Civil Lines, New Delhi

    Nearest Metro Station: Civil Lines

    Cost for two: Rs 1500

    Rating: 4.2

    Timings: 11:00 AM to 12:30 AM

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Diggin, Chanakyapuri, Central Delhi

    Diggin is undoubtedly the best place to visit if you are looking for a Valentine Day dinner in Delhi under budget. This place offers impeccable Italian and Continental on a sweet platter, this cafe is the most classic venue for a memorable date. You can chat for hours, capture cute pictures, and indulge in their popularly sold cheesecakes. Do not miss to order Achari Soya Tikka, Chilli Chicken, Sushi and Dim Sum.

    Where: 11, Santushti Shopping Complex, Race Course Road, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi

    Nearest Metro Station: Lok Kalyan Marg

    Cost for two: 1400

    Rating: 4.2

    Timings: 11:30 AM to 10:00 PM


    Olive Bar & Kitchen, Mehrauli, South Delhi

    Olive Bar & Kitchen was the first fine dining restaurant in a quiet, leafy part of the city. The ambience is romantic with pretty interiors and comfortable seating arrangements. They provide you with both indoor and outdoor seating; opt for an outdoor seating area on a pleasant weather day for valentine’s day dinner. One must have their delicious dishes such as Avocado and Black Quinoa, Sand Lobster Tail, Braised Celeriac and Asparagus, Fisherman’s Catch, Corn Fed Chicken Breast and Cherry Bakewell Tart to have a romantic candlelight dinner.

    Where: One Style Mile, Haveli 6, Kalka Das Marg, Mehrauli, New Delhi

    Nearest Metro Station: Qutub Minar

    Cost for two: Rs 2700

    Rating: 4.0

    Timings: 12:30 PM to 12:30 AM

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    The Saffron Boutique, Sector 104, Noida

    The Saffron Boutique is located in the Sector 104 market. With chic interiors and lighting combined with a splash of white colour; this is an ideal place to visit on Valentine’s Day. They offer mouth-watering North Indian, Chinese and Mughlai dishes. For Valentine’s Day lunch, one should try dishes such as Murgh Bhatti Ka, Mushroom Peshwari, Achari Aloo, Smoked Pineapple with Ice Cream and Affogato Martini.

    Where: 26 & 27 Ashirwaad Complex, Sector 104, Near Sector 110, Noida

    Nearest Metro Station: Botanical Garden

    Cost for two: Rs 800

    Rating: 4 .0

    Timings: 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM 

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Kiyan, The Roseate, New Delhi 

    Situated on NH8, Kiyan at The Roseate is the best place for Valentine’s Day in Delhi, if your partner prefers fancy over everything else. From its luxurious ambience by the poolside to the delectable delicacies, everything here is a heart charmer. While you two can visit here any time of the day, prefer to schedule your date in the evening for a dinner under the starlit sky with a romantic candlelight dinner. This restaurant is the most luxurious and romantic all-day dining in Delhi that serves the best Thai cuisines.

    Where: The Roseate, Samalkha, NH 8, New Delhi

    Cost for two: Rs 2800

    Rating: 4.0

    Timings: All Day Dining

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Impromptu, Golf Course Road, Gurgaon

    Experience sophistication while spending a romantic evening with your better half at Impromptu, Golf Course Road. The eclectic bar menu serves an array of drinks and spirits to offer along with mouth-watering finger food. They also host live music performances which will give you a one-of-a-kind experience and is the best place to go Valentine’s day dinner.

    Where: Vatika Towers Complex, Sector 54, Golf Course Road, Gurgaon

    Nearest Metro Station: Golf Course Road

    Cost for two: Rs 1500

    Rating: 3.8 

    Timings: 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM

    Find the amazing deals and reserve your table for free! Click here


    Mia Bella - Romantic Kitchen, Hauz Khas Village, South Delhi 

    Located in Hauz Khas village, this beautifully decorated restaurant is just perfect for Valentine’s Day Dinner for all the couples out there! There are indoor and outdoor seating arrangements, and also swing seats near the window, which is the most romantic spot. But the best view of the lakeside is reserved for those who manage to get a table on the terrace. The Apple Caramel Sangria is worth-a-try, with Onion Rings and Crispy Fried Chicken Wings with Homemade Sauce.

    Where: 50 E, Hauz Khas Village, New Delhi

    Nearest Metro Station: Hauz Khas

    Cost for two: Rs 1800

    Rating: 3.7

    Timings: 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM
