    Critic Rating

    Sabyasachi Speaks Food & Travel Writer

    3.8/5

    Purely a buffet restaurant Flechazo serves dishes from around the world with a special focus on the Mediterranean and Asian food. The restaurant at Madhapur can accommodate about 250 diners and has a bright décor in blue and white. Flechazo in Spanish means “love at first sight”. The huge buffet has quite a few unique features. At the 'Big O Counter', you can prepare your own pizza with your selection of toppings. The starters are served on the table, and often after gorging on these customers are not able to do justice to the main course items. There is also a live dessert counter where ice creams are made using liquid nitrogen. They have quite a huge spread of other desserts too. Service is extremely quick and personal attention is provided to all guests. The waiters monitor your meal very closely and are sometimes too insistent that you try some more dishes, which may be bothersome to a few people. The pricing of the buffet varies depending on the day of the week as well as the time of the day (lunch or dinner). They have a special rate for late night group dining.

    About Flechazo, Madhapur, Hyderabad


    A multi-cuisine restaurant that is purely based on “MediterrAsian” Concept located in Hitech city, Hyderabad is known by the name “Flechazo”. This casual dining is quite a best place if you are looking for some exciting flavors to relish your mood, soul and of course Taste buds. This casual dining classic eatery is located at Sun Towers, Sector 1, Madhapur, Hyderabad. The outlook of this restaurant is so exceptional that it will give you ecstasy feels and you will fall in love with its interior at the very first sight. The mix and match of electric blue color, rustic wooden furniture, and the rich adaptation of lighting at this dining restaurant create an ambiance that will provide you a dandy atmosphere for sure.

    Located in this Hitech city are famous Ramoji Film City, Birla Mandir, Golconda Fort, Salar Jung Museum, Taj Falaknuma Palace, Charminar, etc that offers you a great sightseeing experience that you can cherish your whole life.

    The popularity of this casual dining restaurant is basically based on the taste of food it offers without stressing your pockets. This uniquely conceptualized outlet offers you a variety of cuisines consisting of North Indian, Pan Asian, and the Mediterranean food pleasing you with every bite you gulp in. If you want to give some amiable flavors to your taste buds, try its 'Coastal Crab Curry’ a special dish that adds magic to a Non-Veg platter. Both Vegetarians, as well as Non-Vegetarians, are being catered here truly well by the attendants till your meal ends and we know that you can’t leave that place without giving them a good feedback.

    Going forward is the major attraction at this outlet, the ‘Big O Counter’ where you can prepare your own Pizza adding your own toppings and sauces. Moving conveyor is also a good sight for you and especially for your kids. It brings your food to you in front of the table making your food preferences easy to handle and carry.  If you think of celebrating any occasion or some special days here, then you will get a surprise Dance performance by the attendants themselves on different songs making your day more refreshing and entertaining.

    Flechazo is not only popular for its food and cuisines being offered but also for the desserts that will bring some divine feels to your gustatory cells. Try the most ravishing ‘liquid Nitrogen Icecream’ that you can try with different flavors to end up your meal on a good flavorful note.
