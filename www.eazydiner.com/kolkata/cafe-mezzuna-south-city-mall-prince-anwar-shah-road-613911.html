    About Cafe Mezzuna, South City Mall, Prince Anwar Shah Road


    Caf Mezzuna is a haven for spirited youngsters and partygoers. This upscale chain restaurant serves an interesting variety of Mediterranean and European cuisine. The caf exhibits modern setting with deep brown wooden panels, green creepers and classy photographs. The seating arrangements are very comfortable thus making it the perfect chill-out zone with gang of friends.

    Savour yummy Mini Burger served in portions of three with delicious Lamb and mixed herbs with a pitcher of ice-cold beer. Health conscious guests can opt for refreshingly hot soups and cold cuts. Do not miss their signature Mezze platter or super tasty herb crusted pan grilled chicken on skewers. Try the signature prawn starter, seafood croquettesand the inhouse pizza. The cocktails wine and colourful mocktails are of good texture and taste. The eclectic menu also includes finger foods and sharing plates to complement with the spirits.

    Luxurious buffet spread, delectable starters, lovely ambience, draft beer, large screens showing exciting live matches and vibrant crowd makes Caf Mezzuna a most desirable place to celebrate special occasions including anniversaries and birthdays. A meal costs INR 1200 for two approximately. The caf is open from 12:30 PM to 11:00 PM

    Located at 224 B, in plush South City Mall, 375, Prince Anwar Shah Road, Kolkata, the caf draws large number of guests especially during weekends. Drinks served in the caf has offers as in one on one free. The service is quite fast and the servers are extremely courteous. They even help you in deciding the menu by suggesting them their best dishes. The portions are good. It is best to book a table in advance to have a happening night at Caf Mezzuna and enjoy exclusive deals. You can book the table without any cancellation charges. Both cash and cards are accepted here.
