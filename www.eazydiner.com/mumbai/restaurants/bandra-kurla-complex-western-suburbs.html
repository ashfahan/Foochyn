    About Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC), Western Suburbs, Mumbai

    Bandra Kurla Complex or BKC as it is commonly known as amongst the locals is counted as Mumbai's trendiest area strewn with numerous fancy restaurants and exciting hip bars. If your taste buds are screaming for an extravagant meal then BKC should be your pit shop. Some of the superstars of this culinary district are:

    Zorawar Kalra extends his father's gastronomic brilliance with this spectacular restaurant that brings together authentic Indian cuisine and modern day techniques. 'Masala Library' is a place worth dropping a hefty bundle for.

    Traditional Cantonese fare served to you in a contemporary glamourous avatar is how we define 'Yauatcha'. It claims to have the best dim sums in town so we suggest you to go and find out for yourself.



    Restaurant deals and offers in Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC), Western Suburbs, Mumbai


    Since welcoming Bandra Kurla Complex to its fold in South Mumbai, the city has seen a dramatic rise in its gastronomic sector. The complex is home to the finest and the most romantic restaurants in the city with access to an extensive variety of global cuisines. Suit up and take your date to one of several glamourous restaurants housed at BKC (that’s how locals call it!). How about some Chinese? Pan Asian? Yes? Try, ‘Yauatcha’, a modern teahouse renowned for the best dim sums in town or ‘Hello Guppy’, a quirky playful eatery dedicated to serving delicious Japanese food. Sink your teeth into buttery naans dipped in spicy curries at sophisticated ‘Jyran’ or ‘Tuskers’ or ‘Maya’. For the contemporary version, give a shot to ‘Masala Library’, a stunning fine dining restaurant serving Modern Indian food. Give your palate an elegant treat by tasting yummy European food at ‘The Good Wife’.
