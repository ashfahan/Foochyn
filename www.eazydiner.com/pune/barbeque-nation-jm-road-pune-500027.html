    3.7/5

    The idea as you know is all about appetizers and grills on the table and then you can serve some mains for yourself, ending with a varied choice of desserts. The mains are the basics like 'Biryani', 'Dal Makhni' and 'Rice'. The meat is well cooked, so it maybe a good idea for meat eaters to be relieved that the curries are decently made. It is in fact like Shaadi ka Khaana where you get mobile starters and appetizers to start your meal with. Some are hits and some are misses. They do not partly roast the veggies or meat for the grill. To grill that and smoke that up, you will need at least 35 minutes to get things rolling. Make sure you ask the boys to partly cook them up and then serve it on the table which will very easily get things going. So, mainly the appetizers are named in a very posh manner as if you were the King and that is the meal you are eating. Classics like 'Prawn Tandoori', 'Chicken Tikka', 'Fish Amritsari' are good and they taste fine. For the mains, you will get some nicely cooked Paneer dish with a choice of Soya or Mushrooms cooked in Hyderabadi style. Stick to 'Dal Makhni' and 'Rice' or maybe the 'Biryani' as it is. Among desserts, try the kulfis which are topped with different types of toppings like 'Paan Masala', 'Oreo Crumbs' and several others.

    About Barbeque Nation, R Deccan Mall, JM Road


    If you have been dreading to host a small get together for friends or even family for it might bankrupt you, your answer lies in the city’s favorite family dining destination ‘Barbeque Nation’. The name is something to bank upon as the restaurant is a safe choice when it comes to large gatherings or lavish dinners. Barbeque Nation has quickly risen in estimation of the diners and today sprawls all over Pune in multiple outlets. The city proudly boasts of five outlets including the one on the third floor of R Deccan Mall, Near Deccan Gymkhana, JM Road. Treat your family or gang of friends to a luxurious feast here. Diners are encouraged to eat to their heart’s content, all very affordable and pocket-friendly. The popularity of Barbeque Nation has put it as one of the best family-style dining restaurants in the country.

    There are two things that puts Barbeque Nation on the map for a foodie – live grills and pre-fix bill. Each table at the restaurant comes fitted with a small live grill, which is later on filled with an assortment of appetizers. Talk, grill and eat what you have prepared. Diners are also encouraged to eat as much as they want for a fixed price. Opt for their lavish buffets and eat till you drop. Barbeque Nation prepares a hearty spread of North Indian, Mughlai and Barbeque dishes. With a chilled beer start gorging on appetizers like, peshawari mutton seekh kebab, charred pineapple, fish tikka, paneer tikka, Cajun spicy baby potato and chicken wings drumstick. Main course gets serious with a delicious affair of curries, oriental gravies and continental dishes.

    Desserts are something to watch out for. Save an appetite for sugar concoctions like, brownie, gulab jamun, ice cream, pudding, pastry and an array of flavoured kulfis chosen from their live kulfi counter. Non-drinkers can always sip on Barbeque Nation’s excellent mocktail spread. Try, spiced tropics, masala margarita, kokum breeze and lemongrass n curry leaf martini. Like every other Barbeque Nation outlet, this one too is decked up in similar welcoming way. The wooden tones are used liberally to infuse warmth to the space. Dining style is informal but comfortable. Pick a corner table if you want a bit of privacy. Barbeque Nation enjoys high reviews for its cozy ambience and a generous gluttonous menu.
